Wildfire Sensor Net Video Demo


What's going on?

We simulated two spots on the local map as sensor locations. We updated our server by heated up the sensors with a blowdryer to denote those sensors as "hot", which would update Android app's hotspots.

If the temperature is too high, those spots are red; else, they are green.

Note that the app was not configured to update in real-time, so we had to close and re-open the app to see updates.

I was the Learning Committee Chair for the Fall 2017 - Spring 2018 academic year. My role was to organize technical workshops to strengthen students’ skillsets.
